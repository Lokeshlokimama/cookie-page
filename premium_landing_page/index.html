Baking Philosophy

We believe a cookie should be an experience, not just a snack. That's why we control temperature, age our dough for 24 hours, and use only top-tier organic raw materials.

The Craft

It's a Science & an Art

Every Crumble & Co. cookie is measured to exactly 6 ounces, rolled by hand, and baked to produce high-top crispy crusts and melted molten centers. We trace our ingredients back to the farms:

  • Single-Origin Cocoa: Sourced ethically from Belgian chocolatier suppliers.
  • Grass-Fed Butter: Pure Irish churned butter with high fat content for a rich flaky crumb.
  • Bourbon Vanilla Bean: Real Madagascar vanilla extract aged to perfection.
  • Organic Cane Sugar: Unrefined sugar preserving rich molasses undertones.
